Volunteer Lines Project

Overview

This project was carried out as my final year project at Northumbria University. The project aim was to design and develop an event planning and management platform that allowed prospective event planners to hire volunteers to assist at their events. This was a simple concept that aimed to reduce the cost for event managers that wanted to host a professional event as this website aimed to provide a professional service without the cost. This was because people would plan their own events and control everything. The build consisted of HTML, CSS, JavaScript & PHP.

The Approach

The project consisted of many different stages that helped to complete the finished product. The stages consisted of some initial user research that was done in the form of a litriture review. Some of the research looked at potential competitors, ways of working and producing design documents.

Once the design documents had been constructed the development phase began. The development was constructed based upon the content of the design documents to ensure consistency is being kept with the considerations of the other research being in mind. Finally, the product was tested using a blackbox testing technique. The product was also user tested with real users. The results of this were written up as the analysis which also took into consideration the project as a whole.
